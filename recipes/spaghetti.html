Spaghetti and Meatball Recipe

Next Recipe

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food experience with our Classic Spaghetti and Meatballs recipe. This timeless Italian-American dish combines perfectly cooked pasta with savory, tender meatballs, all smothered in rich tomato sauce and topped with a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese. It's a culinary journey that brings together the warmth of a family dinner and the bold flavors of Italian cuisine.




Ingredients
  • 1 pound ground beef(or a mixture of beef and pork)
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teapsoon red pepper flakes(optional)
  • Olive oil for frying
  • 1(14 ounces) jar of spaghetti sauce


Instructions

    Make the Meatballs

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine breadcrumbs and milk. Let it sit for a few minutes to absorb the milk.
  2. Add ground beef (or beef and pork mixture), grated Parmesan, chopped onion, minced garlic, chopped parsley, egg, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using) to the breadcrumb mixture.
  3. Mix all the ingredients together until well combined. Be careful not to overmix, as it can make the meatballs tough.
  4. Shape the mixture into 2-inch meatballs. You should get approximately 12 meatballs from this mixture.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Once hot, add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through, about 8-10 minutes. Remove them from the skillet and set them aside.

    6. Cook the Spaghetti

  6. Now while the meatballs are simmering, cook the spaghetti according to the package you have.

    7. Assemble the Dish

  7. Serve the meatballs and sauce over the cooked spaghetti.
  8. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.
  9. Enjoy your homemade spaghetti and meatballs!
